Our Story

Rooted in Sustainability, Growing with Purpose

SouthernRoots Exports was born from a deep connection to the fertile lands of central Nigeria, renowned for producing some of the most potent, oil-rich ginger in the world. This region, home to the Ham people, skilled dedicated farmers, has cultivated ginger as a cornerstone of its agricultural identity for generations.

Recognizing the global demand for high-quality, ethically sourced produce, SouthernRoots was established to share this excellence with the world. What began with ginger has grown to include soya beans, chili pepper, hibiscus, sesame seeds and more, all guided by the needs of our customers and the principles of sustainability.

Driven by quality, rooted in heritage, and committed to fair and transparent trade, SouthernRoots Exports delivers trusted agricultural products from Nigeria to global markets with care for people, the land and the future.

Our Sustainable Practices

From Jaba Land to the World: Ethically Grown, Responsibly Exported

SouthernRoots Exports is committed to preserving Nigeria's agricultural heritage while meeting global demand through sustainable methods:

Regenerative Farming

Our partner farmers in Jaba Land use crop rotation and organic composting to maintain soil fertility for ginger and other crops.

Water Conservation

Rainwater harvesting and drip irrigation systems minimize water usage in our chili pepper and hibiscus farms.

Fair Trade Partnerships

Direct contracts with Ham farmers ensure fair wages and community development in Kaduna.

Organic Certification

Our ginger and soya beans are grown without synthetic pesticides, certified by international organic standards.
